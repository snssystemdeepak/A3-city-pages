An Intuitive Assistant

Your Safety & Security Toolkit

Forward Collision Warning with Active Braking*

Radar sensors help detect if you’re approaching another vehicle too fast, alerting you and applying the brakes if necessary.

Blind Spot Monitoring* with Rear Cross Path Detection*

Receive an alert when other vehicles travel into one of your blind spot zones or when unseen vehicles or obstacles block your path when you’re backing up.

Adaptive Cruise Control with Stop And Go*

With this feature that comes standard on all trims except Base Latitude, you can relieve the pressure of lengthy drives and stop-and-go traffic by automatically adjusting your driving speed to maintain a preselected distance from the vehicle ahead.

Active Lane Management*

When detecting an unintentional lane drift, this system can alert you and correct the vehicle’s position. It also provides steering correction to help avoid collisions with vehicles in your blind spot zones.

Pedestrian and Cyclist Detection with Automatic Braking*

Using sensors at the front of the vehicle, this system can alert you to potential obstacles outside your viewing range. If you don’t respond, the vehicle can automatically engage the brakes to help prevent a collision.

Drowsy Driver Detection*

This system tracks vehicle movement and driver attentiveness and looks for patterns that indicate drowsiness. If drowsiness is detected, the system can send precautionary audible and visual alert signals.

Engineered to Help Protect
